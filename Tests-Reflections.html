Results from Myers-Briggs Test:

Efficient – Commanders see inefficiency not just as a problem in its own right, but as something that pulls time and energy away from all their future goals, an elaborate sabotage consisting of irrationality and laziness. People with the Commander personality type will root out such behavior wherever they go.

Strategic Thinkers – Commanders exemplify the difference between moment-to-moment crisis management and navigating the challenges and steps of a bigger plan, and are known for examining every angle of a problem and not just resolving momentary issues, but moving the whole project forward with their solutions.

Conclusion:

Commanders are natural-born leaders. People with this personality type embody the gifts of charisma and confidence, and project authority in a way that draws crowds together behind a common goal. However, Commanders are also characterized by an often ruthless level of rationality, using their drive, determination and sharp minds to achieve whatever end they’ve set for themselves. Perhaps it is best that they make up only three percent of the population, lest they overwhelm the more timid and sensitive personality types that make up much of the rest of the world – but we have Commanders to thank for many of the businesses and institutions we take for granted every day.

Group reflections

Alessio’s Reflection:

Despite getting off to a rocky start with this project I have found both Anton and Vignesh to be really great to work with. Initially we ran into issues with communication mostly due to our different locations and hence different schedules. Eventually once we were able to get together we began breaking up the assessment into logical pieces and all took leadership to finalize our parts.

Once we had established a clear and concise plan we were able to execute everything relatively quickly and split the workload. I think the shortcoming of our group was the initial barrier of communication and uncertainty of the group which delayed our progress at the start. Another hurdle we initially faced was with choosing an ideal platform to collaborate and start work which was later solved when we decided to stick to basics and use Google docs and Facebook messenger as we all had access.

I learned a bit more about the other members of the group and enjoyed working with them and found we had complimenting skills. Lastly I was surprised of how we were able to come together in a short space of time and rapidly work together to complete this project. I look forward to more group work going forward with Anton and Vignesh and the other tasks in this course.

Anton’s Reflection:

Unfortunately, in the last minute we were notified by admin the team has missed one member, now we only have 3 people in our group, it could be harder for us if we are missing one of the best contributions to our assignment.

However, Vignesh and Alessio has great experience in the IT industry, our problems have been solved in beyond expectation, we have been discussing our project and bring out the best ideas to complete the group assignment.

It is the big goal for all of us to focus on and work closely together to understand each other’s IT skills. Honestly, I am very fortunate to cooperate with these very special members and we are looking forward to achieve the best results and the most enjoyable time for studying IT.

Vignesh’s Reflection:

I believe that both Alessio and Anton were great team members. They contributed their side of the personal information/profile as well as pulled their weight when it came to doing the shared parts of the assignment. The things that worked well were rather than the team just piecing together bits of the assignment and submitting it. We all did our share then collaborated and cross-checked the work on google docs to make sure that we did the best we could.

The things that didn’t work so well were lack of constant communication which could be because we had different schedules and personal commitments. Finally, we hope to take forward what worked and hopefully introduce more methods that’ll help us with the assignments and to improve our communications we just have to set more time and plan ahead to accommodate everyone’s needs.

Overall Group Reflection:

This group had some setbacks as the team was halved when some of the members chose to leave the course/subject but despite that the group worked really well with each other. Although we had difficulty initially setting up a consistent means of communications and basically used RMIT’s canvas emails to communicate with each other which resulted in response from the team members either a day or two later, really slowed our progress but we persisted and the only thing that kept each of us going were our team spirit and motivation. 

Being an introductory course, most of the team were new to online study methods and collaboration which meant the communication isn’t as efficient or to the level required in order to properly share all of our ideas and thoughts on the project and assignment in general. In order to combat this, the group decided to use simple methods of communications such as facebook or just simple messenger applications and google docs which allowed for increased productivity; allowing us to complete this assignment.

For future assignments with this group, the recommendations across the board were starting a little earlier which will give us more time to proof-read and give the assignment final touches to further improve the presentation and general material quality. We wish to continue using simple means of communications and document sharing means in the future assignments to keep things basic.

The most surprising thing that we discovered was that as the university is in Melbourne, one of us lives in Toowoomba, one in country Victoria and the other from Sydney which gave us major doubts about our ability to complete a group based assignment. But as ironic as it may be this introduction to information technology has really shown all of us that for today’s technology long distance means absolutely nothing if we stay determined to complete the task. So, this team's capability has been the most pleasant surprise to us all.
